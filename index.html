I am Narainrit Karuna, a PhD student at Queen's University Belfast

My research primarily focuses on cardiovascular disease and translational research in preclinical models and human studies, particularly diabetic cardiomyopathy and heart failure.

These focus on deciphering the effect of drugs and mechanisms of disease based on transcriptomics (RNA (bulk)-seq/sc-nRNA-seq) and proteomics.

Projects

Investigating impact of drugs on cardiac structure, function and remodelling in context of diabetes induced heart failure.
